# Natural Neighbors

An experiment to map out natural neighborhoods.

Areas that this can encompass include:

1. National:
  1. Parks
  1. Monuments
  1. Forests
  1. Wildlife refuges
  1. Historic sites
  1. Seashores
  1. Lakeshores
  1. Grasslands
  1. Wildernesses
1. State
  1. Parks
  1. Wildlife areas
